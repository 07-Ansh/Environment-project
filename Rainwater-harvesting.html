How It Works

Rainwater is collected from rooftops and stored in tanks. It can then be filtered and used for various purposes, including irrigation and washing.

Benefits of Rainwater

Using rainwater can lower water bills, reduce stormwater runoff, and help maintain healthy gardens without relying on municipal supplies.
